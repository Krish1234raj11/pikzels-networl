About Pikzels Network

Pikzels Network was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to create the most enjoyable Minecraft experience possible. Our team of dedicated developers and staff work tirelessly to bring you unique gameplay features, regular updates, and a welcoming community.

What started as a small survival server has grown into a thriving network with multiple gamemodes and hundreds of daily players from around the world.

Server Rules

General Rules

  1. Be respectful to all players and staff members
  2. No harassment, bullying, or hate speech of any kind
  3. No advertising other servers or services
  4. English only in public chat
  5. No spamming or excessive use of caps

Gameplay Rules

  1. No hacking, cheating, or exploiting bugs
  2. No griefing or stealing
  3. No inappropriate builds or skins
  4. No AFK farming (unless allowed in specific gamemodes)
  5. Follow staff instructions at all times

Discord Rules

  1. Follow all general server rules in Discord
  2. Keep conversations in appropriate channels
  3. No voice channel hopping or ear rape
  4. No @mentioning staff unnecessarily
  5. Discord Terms of Service apply
